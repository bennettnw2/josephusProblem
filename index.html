Josephus Problem

N people (numbered 1 to N) are standing in a circle. Person 1 kills person 2 with a sword and then gives the sword to person 3. Person 3 kills person 4 and gives the sword to person 5. this process is repeated until only one person is alive.

Task: (Medium) Given the number of people N, write a program to find the number of the person that stays alive at the end. (Hard) Show each step of the process.
